Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2010

EduNest was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive. What started as a small school with just 50 students has grown into a vibrant community of over 5,000 learners.

Our journey has been marked by countless success stories, innovative teaching methods, and an unwavering commitment to excellence in education.
